April Brewer Filter Recipe

Brew recommendation

  • Dose: 13g
  • Water: 200g at 93 degrees Celsius
  • Grind size: Medium Course
  • Brew time: 2:20-2:30

Recipe

  1. Begin your brew with an initial 100g pour.
  2. Use your first 30g of the pour to saturate the coffee bed evenly whilst implementing a circular pouring technique.
  3. Continue your pour by completing the final 70g pour into the center of the coffee bed.
  4. Replicate this pouring format once again, commencing your second pour at 35 seconds.
  5. Finish the brew with a total brew time in 2:30-3:00 minutes.
  6. Serve and Enjoy
