Felix Felicis "Liquid Luck"

Ingredients

  • Ashwinder egg
  • Squill bulb
  • Murtlap tentacle
  • Tincture of thyme
  • Occamy eggshell
  • Powdered common rue

Recipe

  1. Add Ashwinder egg to a cauldron, then add horseradish and heat.
  2. Juice a squill bulb, add to the cauldron and stir vigourously.
  3. Chop up an anemone-like growth on the back of Murtlap, then add to mixture and heat.
  4. Add a dash of tincture of thyme and stir slowly.
  5. Grind up Occamy shell and add to mixture.
  6. Stir slowly then add heat to the cauldron.
  7. Add a sprinkle of powdered common rue.
  8. Stir vigorously then heat the cauldron one last time.
  9. Wave wand over potion in a figure of eight (8) and say "Felixempra!"
