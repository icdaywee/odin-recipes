THE ULTIMATE V60 TECHNIQUE BY JAMES HOFFMAN

Recommendation

  • Brew ratio: 60g/L (e.g. 30g per 500mL)
  • Grind size: medium fine
  • Temperature: the hotter, the better (especially with lighter roasts)

    • Recipe

    1. Fill Fellow EKG Gooseneck Kettle with water and set temperature to 210 degree farenheit
    2. Weigh out 20g of light roast coffee from Pair cupworks on the Acaia scale
    3. Using a medium fine grind setting, grind coffee with the Fellow Ode Grinder
    4. Open Hario V60 filter and place into ceramic V60 brewer
    5. Using the now heated up water in the Fellow Kettle, preheat the V60 ceramic
    6. Stir slowly then add heat to the cauldron.
    7. Add a sprinkle of powdered common rue.
    8. Stir vigorously then heat the cauldron one last time.
    9. Wave wand over potion in a figure of eight (8) and say "Felixempra!"
