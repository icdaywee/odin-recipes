Kalita Wave Pourover Recipe

What you'll need

  • Kalita Wave #185 Brewer
  • Kalita #185 paper filter
  • 30g Coffee: Ground medium course
  • Coffee server
  • Gooseneck kettle
  • Water
  • Scale

Steps

  1. Start by filling up your kettle and setting it to boil.
  2. Put a filter in the Kalita Wave, and place on top of a server.
  3. Pour hot water from kettle and rinse filter until you feel heat on the outside of the Kalita, and then discard water
  4. Add 30g of medium course ground coffee to the Kalita wave. Shake to level the coffee bed and zero the scale
  5. Start the timer on the scale and start your pour at the same time.
  6. Pouring is split into six separate pours of 65g of water in 15 seconds*, and allowing to drain for 15 seconds in between each pour
  7. Start each pour in the center. Work your way out and then back to the center, in concentric circles, saturating any dark spots of coffee on the surface.
  8. Coffee should finish draining around 3:30. Remove the Kalita Wave. Serve.
  9. Enjoy
